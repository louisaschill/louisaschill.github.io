About

I’m pursuing my PhD in Computational Neuroscience in the Computational Connectomics Lab at Weill Cornell Medicine. My research bridges neuroscience, network science, and data analytics — exploring how structure and dynamics shape cognition and behavior. I’m broadly motivated by questions at the intersection of biology, computation, and society, and I’m eager to apply my modeling and analytical skills in neuroscience and beyond to solve data-rich, high-impact problems.
