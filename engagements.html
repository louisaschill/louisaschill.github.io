Leadership & Mentorship

Community engagement, teaching, and mentoring initiatives

Machine Learning in Medicine Webinar Series

I co-organize the Machine Learning in Medicine (MLiM) virtual webinar series , an inter-campus collaboration bringing together researchers across Cornell Tech, Weill Cornell, and industry to discuss advances in clinical and biomedical applications of machine learning.

Brain Awareness Day — Weill Cornell

I co-organize Brain Awareness Day , an educational outreach initiative bringing neuroscience to local 4th-grade classrooms through hands-on demonstrations and interactive activities. After reactivating the program in 2024 following its post-pandemic hiatus, our team has since led two successful events — with the next planned for January 2026.

To ensure the program’s long-term sustainability, we established Brain Awareness Day as an official campus organization at Weill Cornell Graduate School, enabling broader student participation and institutional support.

We showcased this outreach work in a presentation to New York State government officials on behalf of Weill Cornell and received the WCGS Student Service & Outreach Award in recognition of leadership in science education and community engagement.
